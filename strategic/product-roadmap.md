### Life Profile (Onboarding Flow)
- Identity and household
- Document upload with smart extraction (26+ document types via Anthropic Claude API)
- Housing details (pre-filled from mortgage statement)
- Income (pre-filled from pay stub, comma formatting)
- Spending (v4 — pre-fill from Life Graph, CSV extraction, custom line items, debt payments category)
- Investments (comma formatting, pre-fill from statements, partner accounts)
- Debt
- Retirement Goals
- Review and confirm

- **Life View** — personalized navigation report (6 sections, generated by Claude)

- **Life Calendar** — full monthly grid, timezone-safe, dual-income payday support
- **Krovos Guide** — AI conversation page with life-phase-aware starter questions
- **Krovos Guide API** — full Life Graph context, white-glove recommendation pattern

## Four Product Surfaces

1. **Life Compass** — Goals first. What matters most right now. Personalized by Financial Mode and life phase.
2. **Life Graph** — Planning center. Document-generated understanding + interactive planning tools.
3. **Life Calendar** — Financial and life events in one place. Auto-populated from Life Graph.
4. **Krovos Guide** — Always-on AI advisor. Knows the complete Life Graph.

## Financial Modes (Behind the Scenes)

| Mode | Trigger |
|---|---|
| Debt-Free | Total non-mortgage debt > $10K |
| FI/FIRE | Savings rate > 25% or retirement age < 55 |
| Family | Has dependents |
| Self-Employed | Self-employment income present |
| Starting Over | Divorce, job loss, or rebuilding indicated |
